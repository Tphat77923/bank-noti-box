Wind Maze Challenge

Toggle the fans to push the ball into the hole before time runs out. Each level introduces a new maze and trickier airflow puzzles. Tap or click on a fan to switch it on or off and watch how the wind changes the ball's trajectory.

Level 1 / 4 Time: 00.0 s

How to play

  • Fans display the direction of their airflow with an arrow. Green fans are blowing, grey fans are idle.
  • Tap a fan to toggle it. Active fans add wind force to the ball based on their arrow direction.
  • The ball carries momentum — plan your timing to avoid walls and navigate corners.
  • Reach the hole before time expires to advance. Clearing all four levels completes the demo.
